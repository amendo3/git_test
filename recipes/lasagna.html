The Best Lasagna Ever!

Description

In this recipe, we will be creating the most tasty and delictable dish of lasagna that you or your guests have ever had!
Make sure to get all of your ingredients in order before beginning, and maybe skim through the steps ahead of time in order to prevent mistakes while cooking!
Once you have everything that you need and are ready to begin, it's time to get started!

Ingredients
  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.
Steps

Some steps require more knowledge, but a basic look at the steps involved is as follows:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
Extra recipe help
How to Layer Our Lasagna

Layering instructions and the order to follow.

  1. Meat sauce. Cover the bottom of the pan.
  2. Noodles. Fill the bottom of the pan with 3-5 noodles depending on pan size.
  3. Ricotta mixture. Spread the layer of noodles with the mixed ricotta cheese.
  4. Mozzarella slices. Scatter slices to allow them to melt together with the ricotta mixture.
  5. Meat sauce. Cover the cheese and noodles with more meat sauce.
  6. Parmesan cheese. Sprinkle some parmesean cheese over the meat sauce.
  7. Repeat the layers as pan allows, and finally top with desired cheeses.
How Long to Cook Lasagna

The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven
preheated to 375 degrees F. Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes,
then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes. Also, it's important to
let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before
you cut into it.
