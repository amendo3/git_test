The Best Fettucine Alfredo Ever!

Description

In this recipe, we will be creating the most tasty and delictable dish of fettucine alfredo that you or your guests have ever had!
Make sure to get all of your ingredients in order before beginning, and maybe skim through the steps ahead of time in order to prevent mistakes while cooking!
Once you have everything that you need and are ready to begin, it's time to get started!

Ingredients
  • 24 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup butter
  • ¾ pint heavy cream
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 dash garlic salt
  • ¾ cup grated Romano cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
Steps
  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Melt butter into cream in a large saucepan over low heat; add salt, pepper, and garlic salt. Increase the heat to medium; stir in grated Romano and Parmesan cheese until melted and sauce has thickened.
  3. Add cooked pasta to sauce and toss until thoroughly coated; serve immediately.
